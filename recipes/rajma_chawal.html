Rajma Chawal

About Rajma Chawal :

Rajma Chawal is a classic North Indian comfort food made with red kidney beans (rajma) cooked in a spicy, flavorful onion-tomato gravy, served with steamed basmati rice (chawal). It's wholesome, protein-rich, and perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ingredients :

For Rajma (Kidney Beans Curry):

  • 1 cup rajma (red kidney beans)
  • 3 cups water (for pressure cooking)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 large tomatoes (pureed or finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

For Chawal (Steamed Rice)

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 2 cups water
  • Salt to taste (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon oil (optional, for non-sticky rice)

Prepration Steps

  1. Soak 1 cup rajma overnight (8–10 hours) in enough water.
  2. Drain and rinse soaked rajma, then pressure cook with 3 cups water and a little salt for 5–6 whistles or until soft.
  3. In a pan, heat oil or ghee and add cumin seeds, bay leaf, cinnamon stick, and cloves.
  4. Once fragrant, add finely chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chili (optional); sauté until the raw smell disappears.
  6. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil starts separating from the masala.
  7. Add turmeric, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt; mix and cook for 2–3 minutes.
  8. Add the cooked rajma along with its water to the gravy.
  9. Simmer on low flame for 15–20 minutes, mashing a few beans to thicken the curry.
  10. Add garam masala and adjust salt or spice if needed.
  11. Meanwhile, rinse 1 cup basmati rice and cook it with 2 cups water, salt, and 1 teaspoon oil until soft and fluffy.
  12. Garnish the rajma with fresh coriander leaves.
  13. Serve hot rajma with steamed chawal for a delicious meal.
